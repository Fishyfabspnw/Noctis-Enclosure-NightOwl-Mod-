# 🔥 Noctis – Multi-Material Heated Enclosure System

A purpose-built heated enclosure designed for high-temperature, multi-material 3D printing.

This is **not** a cosmetic shell.  
This enclosure is engineered for thermal stability, structural rigidity, and reliable multi-color ASA and engineering-grade material output under sustained chamber heat.

---

## 🚀 Overview

The **Noctis-MMU Enclosure** was designed to:

- Maintain elevated chamber temperatures for engineering polymers  
- Support multi-material (MMU) color changes inside a heated environment  
- Keep relative humidity levels low  
- Utilize a PolyDryer-assisted heating strategy  
- Circulate chamber air effectively  
- Integrate environmental sensing  
- Stay simple, modular, and serviceable  

Designed for sustained chamber operation between **60–85°C**.

---

## 🏗 Structural Platform

### Frame Architecture
- Built from **2020 aluminum extrusion**
- Modular panel mounting system
- High rigidity under repeated thermal cycling
- Service-friendly access design

---

## 🧊 Insulated Chamber

- Fully insulated internal chamber
- Designed to retain heat efficiently
- Reduces thermal fluctuation during tool changes
- Improves interlayer adhesion for engineering polymers

Stable temperature = stable print performance.

---

## 🔥 Active Heated Chamber

- PolyDryer-assisted heat input  
- Designed for sustained elevated chamber temperatures  
- Controlled airflow path  

### 🌪 Chamber Circulation

- 5015 blower fans for active air movement
- Eliminates thermal hotspots
- Promotes uniform chamber temperature
- Improves MMU purge consistency

---

## 🧠 Environmental Monitoring

### BME680 Sensor Integration

- Chamber temperature monitoring  
- Relative humidity monitoring  
- VOC / gas level tracking  

Enables:

- Moisture awareness  
- Chamber tuning  
- Data-driven thermal management  

---

## 🎨 Multi-Material Ready

Designed specifically for MMU use in heated environments:

- Stable filament routing under heat  
- Reliable purge performance  
- Controlled chamber environment during color swaps  
- Engineering-grade multi-color support  

---

## 🛠 PTFE Guide Mod

- Dedicated PTFE routing through extrusion structure  
- Prevents binding or misalignment  
- Maintains clean filament path geometry  
- Improves MMU reliability

## 🧩 Panel Options

- Maximum rigidity  
- Industrial durability  
- Excellent heat tolerance  

### Polycarbonate Panels
- High-temp transparency  
- Visual monitoring capability  
- Maintains insulation performance

## 💡 LED Filament Indicator Mod

- Quick color identification
